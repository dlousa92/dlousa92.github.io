About me

I grew up in a small town on the New Jersey side of the Hudson River. I would look accross the water and see New York with its lights and its noise and its big dreams. New York represented adventure, success, and freedom. Something out of reach for a kid from a small town on the New Jersey Side of the Hudson River. For 25 years I never pursued those dreams because I thought I could never reach them. Then one night after a round of board games with friends I had a moment of deep thought. I didn't want to be that kid always destined to look across the river. I could be the designer instead of the player. I could be the creator instead of the spectator. I hope you enjoy some of my creations.

How I Started

The first website I developed was a mock wikipedia page about Brown Bears with little to no styling. From small beginnings come wonderful things. When I first learned about the CSS Box Model my mind was blown away. Then I learned about Flexbox and Grid. Then I learned about Javascript and Dom Interaction. This stuff seemed like wizardry to me just a few months before. It quickly turned into an avalanche of learning and I haven't stopped since.
